Sheet 1; Sum of Profits Based on State!

This is a graph indicating the profits for each of the 50 states. It shows the sum of profits as well as labels the states. Notice that some of the highest profit states are California and New York. We want to look into why and when these profits were the greatest, so that we can inform our business practices in the future.

Sheet 2; This is How New York Has Been Doing!

Notice how New York has a Spike in the most recent year around October, this is especially interesting as last October there was a low point in profit. In order to look into why this may be we should look into how the profits relate to product category.

Sheet 3; New York Profits and Product Category!

Interestingly, New York has made the most profit on Machines, Phones and Binders. This is interesting because, given that the tallest value is phones, we may be able to form a connection between phones coming out in October and the dramatic jump in profit. Next, lets see if we can extrapolate our sales to November of the next year

Sheet 4; Understanding New Yorks Phone Future!

Looking through time we can see that there is consistent jumps in phone sales in the later months. We expect this trend to continue. We do not expect the profits to increase, however, so we should can focus on increasing our sales through phones to hopefully make a big profit.
